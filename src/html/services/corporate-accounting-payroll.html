Why choose us

Secure and integrated

We process clients’ transactions and reporting using secure, enterprise grade financial and reporting software. Our system is a protected Microsoft processing and file sharing environment, which we customize to meet the needs of small or medium sized customers, and integrate with important productivity, reporting, and back office apps.

Better Data and Controls

Our accounting method provides a more controlled process with clear internal checkpoints and more robust datasets which we leverage on your behalf during your accounting cycle. At each step, we can put the right person on the job, to produce better and more streamlined outcomes without overcharging fees.

Analytics Dashboards

Each month, to compliment the monthly reports that you receive and the communications with your Accounting Manager, you also receive access to online PowerBI reports. These reports can be used from any device to check your monthly finances, or search for certain expense items, with just a few clicks.

Software Features

  • easy-use online monthly report dashboards

  • Integrated Digital Expense Management software (for PO’s and reimbursements)
  • Advanced reporting tools (beyond the included basic dashboards)
  • Robust cloud-based accounting reports in near real-time
  • Online HR administration
  • ERP
