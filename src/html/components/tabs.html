About Dezan Shira & Associates

Our services are tailored to help executives and decisionmakers manage the legal, financial, and technological hurdles that international firms face with market entry and operations in Asia's unique markets and jurisdictions.

Our unique, cross-disciplinary advisory model provides clients with deeper insights and expedited knowledge, enabling them to adeptly seize opportunities and navigate change. This advantage empowers them to confidently pursue their goals and secure a competitive edge in Asia.

We are a Pan-Asia practice with fully-integrated corporate offices working both locally and with one another to provide multi-jurisdictional advice and planning for businesses operating across Asia.

We support clients’ operations in Asia from our 27 offices, strategically located throughout China, Hong Kong SAR, India, Indonesia, Mongolia, Singapore, Vietnam, and the United Arab Emirates, as well as from our 14 Dezan Shira Asian Alliance™ offices throughout Australia, Bangladesh, Cambodia, Japan, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, the Philippines, South Korea, Sri Lanka, Thailand, and Türkiye. Together, we are one of Asia’s largest integrated providers of corporate services with more than 40 offices and 600 professionals.

The corporate clients that we serve operate across dozens of industries and originate from more than one hundred countries around the world. Nearly 70% of our customers maintain global headquarters in Europe or the Americas, while Asia-invested firms make up our fastest growing customer segment.

Our practices cater to start ups, mid-cap companies, family firms, listed corporations, and larger multinationals. We work as comfortably with entrepreneurs and owners as with executives and department managers. Our diversified clientele allows us to strengthen the knowledge base that we continue to use in assisting our clients do business across Asia.

The expertise that our professionals apply stems from supporting clients in the region for more than 30 years. Our versatile depth of knowledge and strong practice familiarity helps us better serve our clients’ present and future needs within Asia’s developing markets

To support companies’ investments and operations in Asia, we provide core expert advisory and corporate services through Dezan Shira & Associates, as well as regional thought leadership and business knowledge and market intelligence, and specialized applications and services through our brands and subsidiaries.

We take pride in our family of brands, through which we offer specialized solutions and knowledge that empowers businesses as they journey towards strategic growth and operational excellence across the diverse landscapes of Asia's business environment.
