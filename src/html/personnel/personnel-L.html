Alberto oversees global operations and expansion, managing four South China offices (Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Zhongshan, Hong Kong) and all ASEAN branches. He also leads the firm’s development in overseas markets, including liaison offices in the US and Europe.

Bio

Alberto Vettoretti was born in Italy and received his education in the UK where he obtained a bachelor’s degree in Chinese, Economics, and Management from the University of Durham and an MSc with a specialization in Chinese law from the University of Staffordshire.

He has been living in Asia for over 26 years and speaks and writes fluent Mandarin, Italian, English, and Spanish.

Alberto joined Dezan Shira & Associates as an equity partner in September 1998. Over the years, the company has grown into one of the largest, privately-held pan-Asia professional organization with over 600 professionals and 35 offices. 

In 2014, he led the firm’s expansion in Asia through the establishment of the Dezan Shira Asia Alliance network with partners and offices in Australia, Bangladesh, Cambodia, Japan, Malaysia, Nepal, Russia, the Philippines, South Korea, Sri Lanka and Thailand. Dezan Shira & Associates and its Asia Alliance are at the forefront of professional services in the region and represent one of the largest and longest established professional networks in Asia.
