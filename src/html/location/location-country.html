Dezan Shira in China

Already the world’s center of manufacturing, and featuring the largest transportation and supply chain logistics networks in the world, China’s rising purchasing power, expanding middle class, and 1.4 billion person population, has it set to become the largest retail market in the world as well.

As a market for investment, increasing disposable income, rising by 6.1 percent in 2023 to reach RMB 39,218 (US$5,451), allows many foreign companies to produce goods specifically for local consumption, rather than use the country as a production base for export-led manufacturing—making China their largest market for growth. 

Despite ongoing concerns about consumer confidence, the Chinese government has identified domestic demand as a priority for the nation’s economy, highlighting a government commitment to stimulating consumption and investment to bolster economic growth.

PEO (Professional Employer Organizations) help companies to onboard staff in Asia rapidly, in full compliance with local laws, and without the investment required to set up and operate an overseas legal entity.
