About Us

About Dezan Shira & Associates 

Dezan Shira & Associates is a pan-Asia, multi-disciplinary professional services firm - providing clients with corporate, professional, and technology services essential for navigating Asia's complex regulatory environments. Founded in 1992, the firm is in its fourth decade of operation, and has grown to include dozens of technical practices located across Asia.

We support clients from more than 40 offices, including 27 strategically located throughout China, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Malaysia, Mongolia, Singapore, Vietnam, and Dubai, and 13 Asian Alliance partner offices based in Australia, Bangladesh, Japan, the Philippines, Thailand, Sri Lanka, Nepal, and South Korea. With more than 600 professionals to serve you, we are one of Asia’s largest integrated corporate services providers.
