Professional services for Asia's markets

Our services are tailored to help executives and decisionmakers manage the legal, financial, and technological hurdles that international firms face with market entry and operations in Asia's unique markets and jurisdictions.

Our unique, cross-disciplinary advisory model provides clients with deeper insights and expedited knowledge, enabling them to adeptly seize opportunities and navigate change. This advantage empowers them to confidently pursue their goals and secure a competitive edge in Asia.

As one of the leading firms for registering foreign-owned businesses in Asia, we are equipped to advise clients regarding the optimal options for structuring a company to meet their objectives. Our teams advise on all matters relating to establishment, cross- border asset transfers, relocation, as well as mergers and acquisitions.

Our Corporate Secretarial services are designed to provide companies with the reliable support needed for them to know with confidence that their entities in Asia remain in compliance, and are several steps ahead of internal and regulatory changes. PEO (Professional Employer Organizations) help companies to onboard staff in Asia rapidly, in full compliance with local laws, and without the investment required to set up and operate an overseas legal entity.

As a strategy, this allows a company to test market entry into one or more Asian countries, and then retain the option to easily scale back, switch to a different country, or upon proven success in the market, to proceed in establishing a formal presence.
