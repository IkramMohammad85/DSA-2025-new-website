Navigating Annual Audit in China: Why Every Company Should Perform One Annually

Posted On November 2023
►

Are you performing an audit of your Chinese business annually? Even though not mandated by law, there are many compelling reasons every company in China should perform an annual audit.

Find out the benefits of doing so for your business, from meeting legal requirements to boosting overall financial health. Discover too what happens during these audits and why certain elements, like internal controls and related party transactions, are crucial to running operations abroad.

In this short interview, Ivy Gu, Manager in our Audit and Corporate Accounting Services team, will share her seasoned expertise, guiding you towards making astute decisions for your business within the dynamic China landscape.
