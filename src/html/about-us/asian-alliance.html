Asian Alliance

Seamless solutions across Asian markets

Since 2015, Dezan Shira & Associates has led a regional network of like-minded and trusted professional services firms — known as the Asian Alliance. This alliance extends our footprint through member firms in Australia, Bangladesh, Japan, the Philippines, Thailand, Sri Lanka, Nepal, and South Korea.

United by a shared mission to guide foreign investors through Asia’s complex regulatory landscape. Our alliance ensures consistent, high-quality service delivery across jurisdictions. Each member upholds the same standards in professional expertise, client support, and digital infrastructure — providing a seamless experience for clients operating across multiple Asian markets. Together, we form one of the region’s largest integrated corporate services networks, with 40 offices and 600 professionals across Asia.
