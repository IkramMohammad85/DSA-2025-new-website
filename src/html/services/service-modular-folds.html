Building Performance within the ESG Landscape  

Stakeholders are increasingly scrutinizing environmental, social and governance (ESG) performance—and looking for companies to rise to the challenge of delivering ESG improvements. 

It is becoming increasingly expected, or required by law, that companies produce ESG performance reports that adhere to finance-grade standards, to satisfy corporate investors, and industry and country governing bodies.  

Yet implementing real-world models for governance is just beginning for many organizations, which is why we provide customized advisory on ESG environments for clients across Asian markets, assisting clients in developing their global strategies, together with local execution to help deliver guidance on ESG strategy through to complete ESG compliance and reporting systems.
