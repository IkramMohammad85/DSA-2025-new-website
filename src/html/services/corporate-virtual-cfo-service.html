Corporate Virtual CFO Service

Strategic finance leadership, without the cost of a full-time CFO

Financial oversight is critical as companies navigate Asia’s complex growth environments—but full-time CFOs may not always be practical or cost-effective. Dezan Shira & Associates’ Virtual CFO (vCFO) service offers on-the-ground financial leadership without the overhead.

Our vCFOs oversee budgeting, tax, cash flow, reporting, and special projects—providing both interim and ongoing support while aligning local operations with global financial strategy. Backed by in-house tax, audit, legal, HR, and IT expertise, vCFOs bridge functions, deliver objective financial assessments, and ensure compliance across jurisdictions. Businesses gain expert guidance to strengthen financial performance, manage complexity, and scale with confidence—without the burden of permanent hires.

Advantages of a Virtual CFO

Strategic support for evolving business needs

A vCFO flexibly supports transitions, projects, or internal teams, delivering expert oversight tailored to your business stage and evolving priorities.

Cost-effective alternative to full-time hires

Gain CFO-level insights without the cost of full-time hires. Our vCFO model provides senior expertise, backed by our cross-functional advisory teams.

Local insight with global alignment

Our vCFOs bring local compliance expertise and align regional operations with group-level strategies - facilitating coordination across the business.
