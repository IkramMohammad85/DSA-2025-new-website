Are you up to date with the rapidly evolving Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) landscape in Southeast Asia? How can your business ensure compliance with diverse and fast-developing regional regulations?

As ESG practices gain global traction, the ASEAN region is no exception. To strengthen ASEAN’s position as a leading investment hub, the ASEAN Exchanges—including Bursa Malaysia, Indonesia Stock Exchange, Philippine Stock Exchange, Singapore Exchange Group, Vietnam Exchange, and the Stock Exchange of Thailand—are working together to enhance the capital markets. This collaboration focuses on promoting sustainability and driving sustainable investments while creating interconnected products to broaden investment opportunities.

With increasing regulatory pressures, greater investor scrutiny, and rising public expectations, understanding the ESG transition priorities across these key ASEAN markets is essential for businesses seeking compliance and growth.

Our publication, "Unlocking the ASEAN-6 | ESG Transition Overview 2024", provides a comprehensive analysis of the latest sustainability reporting requirements across these six critical ASEAN economies. It equips investors with the insights they need to ensure compliance and maximize business value through quality ESG disclosures.

Additionally, the guide compiles valuable resources from Asia Briefing to help businesses refine their strategies and planning for ESG compliance and sustainable growth in Southeast Asia.

Download the publication to access in-depth analysis and practical tools that will support your ESG objectives and enhance your business compliance in the ASEAN region.
