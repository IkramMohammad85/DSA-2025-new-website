About Us

Your source for Asia market commentary 

Dezan Shira & Associates is a leading professional services firm with over three decades of experience guiding international companies through Asia’s complex regulatory, tax, and investment environments. Our partners, legal and tax specialists, and industry experts are regularly quoted across global business media for their in-depth understanding of local markets.

We offer journalists and media professionals access to commentary, background briefings, and expert analysis on topics including foreign investment trends, regulatory developments, trade policy, compliance, tax reform, and doing business in Asia.

For timely insights or interview requests, our media team will connect you with the most relevant spokesperson across our offices in Asia, Europe, and North America.
